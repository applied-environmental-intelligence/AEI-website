01 / The platform

We measure the Earth beneath what you build.

Ground-based sensing is our edge. We operate dense networks of seismic and distributed acoustic sensors, fuse them with satellite imagery, and run physics-based models. Together, they image the subsurface and surface as conditions change. Geodetic networks join in v2.0 through a sensing partner.

02 / The difference

Most tools see half the picture. We model the whole system.

The field is split between ground-sensor companies and satellite companies, and nearly all of them are purely data-centric. AEI fuses both and adds the physics. That's why our picture holds together where single-source, data-only tools fall apart.

The status quo
-Ground sensors or satellite, rarely fused
-Purely data-centric, with no physical model of the Earth
-Siloed data streams and siloed analysis
-Insight locked behind deep, scarce expertise
-Black-box outputs that are hard to audit or trust
-Backward-looking snapshots and static reports
The AEI approach
+Ground and satellite, fused into one coherent record
+Physics-based models, grounded in earth science
+Uncertainty quantified on every output
+Transparent, continuously evaluated pipelines
+Expertise translated into decision-ready language
+A living model that assimilates new data and projects forward
03 / Capabilities

Four sensing modalities, one model of the ground.

Seismic

Ground motion & micro-seismicity

Dense arrays catch the tremors, slips, and vibrations that precede failure long before they are felt.

Geodeticv2.0 · with partner

Surface deformation & strain

Millimeter-scale movement of the ground surface, including subsidence, uplift, and creep. Joining the platform in v2.0 through a sensing partner.

Distributed acoustic sensing

Every meter of fiber, a sensor

Existing fiber routes become continuous acoustic arrays with kilometers of coverage and no new hardware in the ground.

Satellite

Landscape-scale context

Optical and radar imagery ties local sensing to wider terrain with broad coverage and change over time.

04 / The pipeline

From noisy signal to a decision you can defend.

Field data is messy, disaggregated, and incomplete. Our pipelines are agentic but never opaque. They are grounded in earth science, robust to noise, and auditable end to end, with uncertainty carried through every step. So you can take the output to a regulator, board, or engineering team.

STEP 01

Sense

Continuous data from ground networks and satellites streams in.

STEP 02

Fuse

Sources are aligned in space and time into one coherent record.

STEP 03

Model

Physics-based models infer what's happening beneath the surface.

STEP 04

Image

Results render as live imagery of the ground and its motion.

STEP 05

Decide

A traceable alert or assessment, with the evidence attached.

Transparent

Every result traces back to the raw signal that produced it.

Uncertainty-quantified

Confidence travels with the answer, never a number without a margin.

Robust to noise

Built for real field conditions with gaps, drift, and bad sensors included.

Continuously evaluated

Evaluations are a core principle, not an afterthought. We measure what we ship.

AI is the instrument, not the decision-maker. We build augmented intelligence. The human expert stays at the center while the machine carries deep geophysical reasoning in language people can act on. It lowers the barrier between science and the call and helps reskill teams instead of black-boxing them.
