02 / The difference

Most tools see half the picture. We model the whole system.

The field is split between ground-sensor companies and satellite companies — and nearly all of them are purely data-centric. AEI fuses both and adds the physics. That's why our picture holds together where single-source, data-only tools fall apart: under noise, at the edges, and when a decision actually has to be defended.

03 / Capabilities

Three sensing modalities, one model of the ground.

Each layer is independently strong. Together they resolve motion, strain, and structure that any single source would miss.

Seismic

Ground motion & micro-seismicity

Dense arrays catch the tremors, slips, and vibrations that precede failure — long before they're felt.

Distributed acoustic sensing

Every meter of fiber, a sensor

Existing fiber routes become continuous acoustic arrays — kilometers of coverage, no new hardware in the ground.

Satellite

Landscape-scale context

Optical and radar imagery ties local sensing to the wider terrain — wide area, repeat coverage, change over time.

04 / The pipeline

From noisy signal to a decision you can defend.

Field data is messy, disaggregated, and incomplete. Our pipelines are agentic but never opaque — grounded in earth science, robust to noise, and auditable end to end, with uncertainty carried through every step. So the output is something you can take to a regulator, a board, or an engineering team.

STEP 01

Sense

Continuous data from ground networks and satellites streams in.

STEP 02

Fuse

Sources are aligned in space and time into one coherent record.

STEP 03

Model

Physics-based models infer what's happening beneath the surface.

STEP 04

Image

Results render as live imagery of the ground and its motion.

STEP 05

Decide

A traceable alert or assessment, with the evidence attached.

Transparent

Every result traces back to the raw signal that produced it.

Uncertainty-quantified

Confidence travels with the answer — never a number without a margin.

Robust to noise

Built for real field conditions — gaps, drift, and bad sensors included.

Continuously evaluated

Evaluations are a core principle, not an afterthought — we measure what we ship.

AI is the instrument here, not the decision-maker. We build augmented intelligence — the human expert stays at the center while the machine carries deep geophysical reasoning to them, in language they can act on. It lowers the barrier between the science and the call, and re-skills teams instead of black-boxing them.
